About Inclusive

How we are connecting unbanked Africans to the global economy

Inclusive is a Pan-African identity verification API that helps businesses (mainly financial institutions,fintechs & developers) figure out ,if their customers, are who they say they are or not, via digital channels.

We do this by connecting to third party trusted agencies(ID databases) across Africa, into a single API ,encrypting & hashing identities on the blockchain and placing access to customer data in the hands of the identity owner.

Our mission is to ensure Africans enjoy the privacy, security and economic value of their identities

We believe opening up access to the global economy, begins with identity and being able to build financial stories around people.
